About me

Hi there! My name is Stephen Dawe and I am a recent recipient of the BASc in Chemical Engineering at The University of Toronto. I grew up in Oakville, Ontario. I moved to Downtown Toronto in September 2012 to start my undergrad, and I've lived here ever since. My hobbies include videogames, barbershop quartet, theatre acting, sketch comedy, and more! I've been cast for the last two showings of Skule Nite, the annual U of T engineering sketch comedy musical revue. As far as professional development goes, I'm building a startup this summer at The Hatchery, a startup incubator aimed at undergraduate students with an idea and an entrepreneurial spirit. I'm developing a heated/battery extending phone case to prevent phone shutdown in cold temperatures. Also I'm taking a coding bootcamp.
